Pay by Bank Account

While paying by bank transfer (ACH) is less expensive, it is more complicated to setup. After you enter your information your account has to be "verified" by entering in the amount of two very small deposits that are made for this purpose. So you enter your information here, check your online bank account after a few days, and come back and enter the amounts you see (they will have a name of "Verification"). Once the bank is verified on your account though it is simple to make changes to your subscription if you need to. Can only accept US banks.

So perhaps I am delusional (my wife would agree with you if you thought "yes"), but I imagine there are a handful of people out there that are so very excited about what I'm doing here that they don't think the modest amount I am charging is enough, and want to give more so that I will be able to DO more with this project. If you are one of those rare (imaginary?) people then you can pay whatever you would like, and I'll try to come up with some substantial way to thank you for your generosity
