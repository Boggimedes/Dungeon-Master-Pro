  • About GameMaster.Pro

    GameMaster.Pro is just me: Daniel Boggs. I designed, developed, tested, bled, cried, screamed, and finally published this tool hoping to make tabletop games around the world a little better. It would be great if it took off and I eventually had a few thousand users, but even a few hundred would be fun. No matter what I'm going to keep working on it, because I use it myself for my games!

  • Why Charge/Why Charge So Little

    I'm expecting people may ask why I am charging for this tool, or why I am charging such a small amount. I thought about trying to keep this going as a free tool, or as something that is ad supported, but the processing requirements are surprisingly high so if I get very many users the server costs will start adding up. The reson I didn't do ad supported is because I focused on using every INCH of screen space to its fullest. Ads would just destroy that. Finally, why so little? Why less than $3 a month? Well because I don't want to get rich off this thing. I'd rather more people use it than charge a hefty fee. In fact if you can't afford the monthly fee email me at contact@bwa-designs.com and we'll work somethign out for you.
