Combat Board

TL;DR;
  • Plus at the top left opens quick add to toss a generic creature on the board
  • Add Group to add a group of monsters or players
  • Start Combat resets to round 1 and sorts by initiative
  • Button on the far left ends turn for that character/monster
  • Right click anywhere BUT HP for context menu
  • Right click on HP for quick hitpoint increase decrease

Monsters/Characters (Actors) are added by the quick menu ("+" in upper left) or by the "Monster" side bar (seen in step 1 of tutorial). This combat board tracks everything you would expect: Status, HP, Initiative, Round, etc. Plus has additional information close at hand like AC and Initiative. The selected row will also show more details in the combat details section (covered later)

The buttons at the top are "+" used to quickly add a generic Actor, "Add Group" to add a group of Actors to the combat board, "Clear" which will clear the combat board, and "Start Combat" which resets to the first round and orders by initiative.

there are also right click context menus. If you right click anywhere OTHER THAN the HP field you will get a menu including various actions like saving throws for actor or the whole table, remove actor from the table, or adding a persistent effect (ongoing damage, status effect, etc). If you right click on the HP field it gives you a quick menu for adding or subtracting HP. Try them out!
